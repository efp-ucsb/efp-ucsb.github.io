Economic Impact of Cannabis in Santa Barbara County and across the US

Additional Information:

  • As of July 2019, 33 states (and Washington DC) have legalized medical marijuana and 11 states (and Washington DC) have legalized Recreational marijuana.
  • All but 2 states have legalized either medical marijuana or CBD oil for medical use.
  • In California, if a person or business is caught growing Cannabis without a license, they will be charged $10,000 per day until the plants are cut down (up to $900,000); after 90 days a lien can be placed on the property
  • After Colorado legalized recreational marijuana in 2012, they saw record tourism numbers, a huge drop in unemployment, and a significant home value increase
  • Santa Barbara County's relatively relaxed laws when it comes to cannabis cultivation has lead to a huge rush of growers with some of the largest cannabis farms in the world. Santa Barbara County has the most active cannabis cultivation licenses in the state.
