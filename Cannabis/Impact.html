Economic Impact of Cannabis in Santa Barbara County and across the US

Impact Study Preliminary Report:

This Impact report is very preliminary due to the limited Information we were able to gather. This report will be updated as new information becomes available. This report analyzes the impact that the legal Cannabis industry has on the County of Santa Barbara. We made this report with professional economic modeling software using data from local cannabis vendors and the County of Santa Barbara. This report is made solely to analyze economic impact and does not represent the views of the University of California Santa Barbara, the University of California system, or any EFP team members.
