Santa Barbara South County Economic Summit
May 16, 2019 -- Granada Theater, Santa Barbara
In our annual event, we will provide an update to the community on the current economic trends in the County. This year's speakers will discuss a variety of topics, including how current monetary policy affects the economy, the impact of financial innovation and bank regulations on local industries, and the state of the Santa Barbara County housing market.

    Speakers:
  • Neel Kashkari, President, Federal Reserve Bank of Minneapolis
  • Kathie Moe, Regional Director, FDIC
  • Michael Cox, Faciam Holdings; CFO El Capitan Advisers
  • Peter Rupert, UCSB Economics and EFP
