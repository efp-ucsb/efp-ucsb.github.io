Santa Barbara South County Economic Summit -- May 16, 2019 -- View Slides

May 16, 2019 -- Granada Theater, Santa Barbara
In our annual event, we provided an update to the community on the current economic trends in the County. This year's speakers discussed a variety of topics, including how current monetary policy affects the economy, the impact of financial innovation and bank regulations on local industries, and the state of the Santa Barbara County housing market. Santa Barbara County Mid-Year Report
January 27, 2019 -- Alisal Ranch
In this annual event, we provided an update of the state of the Santa Barbara County's North Coast, including a robust discussion of the "four S's": the Shutdown, Slowdown, Smackdown, and Showdown. In our discussion, we touch on the economic impact of the government shutdown, explore whether there really is a growth slowdown, present potential impacts of the US-China trade war, and highlight the issues surrounding Califronia's pension obligations.

    Speakers:
  • Peter Rupert, UCSB Economics and EFP

  • Jason Stillwell, City Manager, Santa Maria

  • Jim Throop, City Manager, Lompoc

  • Marc Bierdzinski, City Manager, Buellton

  • Brad Vidro, Former City Manager, Solvang

  • Rick Haydon, City Manager, Solvang
